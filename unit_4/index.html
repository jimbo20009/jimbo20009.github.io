Harvest Festival Hoedown XXII

It's a hoot!

DC Lambda Squares is proud to sponsor the twenty-second annual Harvest Festival Hoedown at the Yorktowne Hotel in York, Penn. It will be held on Veterans Day weekend, Nov. 11-13, 2016 and feature three of the nation's top square dance callers. Key features of the weekend:

  • A hundred or so friends from near and far
  • 17 hours of dancing in three halls
  • Mainstream, Plus, Advanced, Challenge 1, and Challenge 2 programs of Modern Western Square Dancing
  • An elegant hotel of another time, updated with modern amenities
  • Original spring-loaded hardwood dance floors, constructed in 1925
  • Door prizes, 50/50 raffles, and our famous basket raffle
  • Ice cream social on Saturday evening
